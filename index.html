Weather that understands altitude

Valley forecasts aren't summit forecasts. Every fell gets its own altitude-adjusted prediction; temperature, wind, visibility, and a simple safety rating so you know before you go.

Core Feature

Know every fell

Height, grid reference, region, weather conditions, an interactive map, and the best routes up. Everything you need for each of the 214 Wainwrights, all in one place.

Pro Feature

Which fell this weekend?

The app scores your remaining fells by weather, distance, daylight, and whether you can bag multiple summits in one walk. It tells you not just where is best to go, but when is best to start.
