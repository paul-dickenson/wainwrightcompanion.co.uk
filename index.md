# The Wainwright Companion

**Track, navigate and complete all 214 Wainwright fells in the Lake District.**

The definitive companion for Wainwright baggers. Whether you're ticking off your first fell or chasing your final summit, this app keeps track of your progress, guides you on the hills, and helps you plan your next walk.

## Features

### Complete Checklist
All 214 fells from Alfred Wainwright's seven Pictorial Guides. Filter by region, difficulty, or completion status. Search by name. Track repeat ascents with dates, notes, ratings, and photos.

### GPS Navigation
Follow walking routes with turn-by-turn guidance. Off-route alerts warn you at 50m, 100m, and 200m. A live breadcrumb trail records your path, and a walk summary captures your distance, ascent, and time.

### Interactive Map
See all 214 fells colour-coded by region. Zoom out for region clusters showing your progress at a glance. Zoom in to see individual summits, routes, and nearby car parks.

### Weather & Safety
Summit-specific weather forecasts with altitude-corrected temperature and wind. Clear safety ratings — green, amber, or red — so you know conditions before you set off.

### Smart Suggestions
Weather-ranked recommendations for the week ahead. The app scores each unbagged fell by forecast conditions, daylight hours, and nearby fells you could combine into a single walk.

### Statistics & Achievements
Progress rings, charts, and milestones. Track your highest, lowest, and most recent bags. Unlock achievements as you progress — from First Steps to completing all seven books.

### Social
Share completions with friends. Follow other Wainwright baggers and see their progress. Compare your journey with the community.

### Built for the Hills
- Works offline — cached weather, bundled routes, local data
- Syncs across your devices via iCloud
- Battery-adaptive GPS for long days out
- Walks saved as Hiking workouts in Apple Health
