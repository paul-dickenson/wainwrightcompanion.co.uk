App Support

Questions about The Wainwright Companion? We're happy to help.

Common Questions

  • Your completion data is stored on your device and synced automatically via iCloud — make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID on all your devices
  • GPS navigation and weather features require a Companion Pro subscription
  • To restore a previous purchase, go to Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases
  • To delete your account and all data, go to Settings → Delete Account
  • Routes are pre-loaded and work offline — no signal needed once the app is installed
  • Weather forecasts require an internet connection; the last fetched forecast is shown when offline
